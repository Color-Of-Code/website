# Derivatives

* $c$ is a constant
* $f$, $g$ are functions of a variable $x$

## Basic properties

### Product rule

$(f.g)'=f'.g + g'.f$

### Quotient rule

$(\frac{f}{g})'=\frac{f'.g-g'.f}{g^2}$

### Chain rule

$(f \circ g)'=f' \circ g . g'$

## Polynomials

$\frac{d}{dx}(c)=0$

$\frac{d}{dx}(x)=1$

$\frac{d}{dx}(x^n)=n.x^{n-1}$
